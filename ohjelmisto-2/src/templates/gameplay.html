Game Instructions

The purpose of the game is to accumulate as much CO2 emissions as possible in the span of 24 months.

To answer a question click one of the six buttons with a country name on it.

After answering correctly, choose where to travel from the remaining options - the further you go, the more emissions you get.

Wrong answers will remove your chosen option and a random option.

Use lifelines to remove half of the answer options.
